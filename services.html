Our Services

Comprehensive Solutions Tailored to Your Industry Needs

Mullen Equipment offers a wide range of services designed to support your business from initial consultation to after-sales support. With decades of expertise in industrial and chemical equipment, we go beyond just providing products—we deliver full-service solutions that help your operations run smoothly and efficiently. Here’s how we can support your business:

Equipment Sales & Distribution

We represent top-tier manufacturers and provide a broad portfolio of industrial and chemical equipment. Our product offerings include process equipment, pumps, filtration systems, valves, storage tanks, and more, ensuring you receive the highest quality and most reliable solutions tailored to your specific industry needs.

Industries Served:
  • Chemical
  • Pharmaceutical
  • Petrochemical
  • Food & Beverage
  • Paper & Pulp
  • Municipal Services
Customized Equipment Solutions

Every industry has unique requirements, and we specialize in providing customized equipment solutions to meet those needs. Our team collaborates closely with clients to design, source, and implement tailored solutions that fit their operational goals, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency.

Key Offerings:
  • Custom process equipment
  • Specialized configurations
  • Turnkey systems integration
Technical Consultation & Project Support

Our experienced technical team is here to assist with project planning, equipment selection, and system design. We offer expert consultation services to help you choose the right products, optimize your processes, and ensure compliance with industry regulations.

Services Include:
  • Product selection guidance
  • System design and configuration
  • Compliance and safety reviews
Installation & Commissioning

We provide end-to-end support for equipment installation and commissioning. Our trained technicians ensure that your new equipment is installed correctly and operates efficiently from day one. We handle everything from on-site setup to operational testing, minimizing downtime and ensuring a smooth start-up.

Installation Services:
  • On-site assembly and setup
  • Operational testing and calibration
  • Staff training and operational guidance
After-Sales Support & Maintenance

Your partnership with Mullen Equipment doesn’t end after the sale. We offer ongoing support and maintenance services to keep your equipment running at peak performance. Our responsive team provides troubleshooting, parts replacement, and preventative maintenance, ensuring maximum uptime and efficiency.

Support Services:
  • 24/7 technical support
  • Preventative maintenance programs
  • Spare parts and replacement components
Training & Education

We believe in empowering your team with the knowledge they need to operate equipment safely and effectively. We offer training sessions tailored to your specific equipment, covering operation, maintenance, and safety procedures.

Training Options:
  • On-site and virtual training
  • Customized training programs
  • Equipment safety and compliance

About Mullen Equipment

Based in Troy, Michigan, Mullen Equipment has been a successful industrial equipment representative and chemical equipment distributor in the Midwest since 1950. Generating significant sales for our principals in the Chemical industry products sector, Pharmaceutical process solutions, Petrochemical industry, Industrial sector services, Municipal solutions, Food & Beverage, Paper & Pulp and various other industries. Today, we continue to expand our reach and areas of expertise through excellent service to our customers and principals.
