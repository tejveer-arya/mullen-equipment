ABOUT MULLEN EQUIPMENT CORPORATION

Founded in 1950, Mullen Equipment started as a small industrial equipment distributor. Over the years, the company faced numerous challenges, from economic recessions to shifts in industrial technology. Yet, through it all, they not only survived but thrived by maintaining a clear focus on two key principles: innovation and customer service. When Ed Kmit took over the company from founder Bill Mullen in 1983, Mullen Equipment was already a trusted name in the Midwest. But instead of resting on their laurels, Ed led the company through a transformative phase, expanding their product range and adopting cutting-edge technologies such as infrared thermal systems and advanced waste water treatment solutions.
